Data Visualization Vogue

Data visualization piqued my interest when I discovered the work of data designer Giorgia Lupi. In the spring semester of 2020, I tacked a data visualization independent study onto my course load and immediately knew that I wanted to experiment with wearable data. Because of the many quips that my friends and loved ones make about me changing my outfit many times throughout the course of a day, I spent a week collecting data pertaining to my personal vanity.
