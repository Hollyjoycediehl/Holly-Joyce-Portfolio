White Ink Poster

Illustrating and Printing White Inks on Black Paper

Printing With White

Advances in digital printers allow designers to create pieces that would have before only been possible through manual and or expensive alternatives. The HP InkJet capabilities allowed me to print white ink onto the black poster substrate. To correctly print the poster, I created a white underbase in Illustrator and used the separation capabilities (the same process as in screen printing) to separate the layers for the printer. The poster layed down 3 passes of white color on the paper and then printed CMYK over top of the white ink.
