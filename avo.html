Adding Whimsy to Mail

InDesign’s variable data capabilities allowed each of the Avo’s mailers to target the customer individually. After gathering the answers on 17 Google surveys, a Google Sheets documented the customer's name, ideal mealtime, and avocado eating preference. The data transferred onto a .csv form which changed the data on the InDesign file and created seventeen mailers with unique names, calls-to-action, and images.

Avo is an avocado bistro concept that I fabricated for the purposes of this project. Disney Imagineer and animator Mary Blair’s color palettes inspired the choices in colors of the playful illustrations. Custom folding and die cutting was added to create a swing effect to add extra whimsy and fun to the customer’s overall experience.
